Our full-day workshop [Multi-Modal Spatial AI for Robust Navigation and Open-World Understanding](https://xingxingzuo.github.io/MM-SpatialAI/) has been accepted at ICRA 2026! The workshop will be held on June 1, 2026 in Vienna, Austria, featuring keynote talks from Alex Wong (Yale), Hermann Blum (Bonn), Dezhen Song (MBZUAI), Timothy D. Barfoot (Toronto), Margarita Chli (ETH Zurich), Sebastian Scherer (CMU), and Andrew Davison (Imperial College London). We welcome submissions of research papers, field reports, and visionary papers on topics including multi-modal SLAM, learning-based pose estimation, neural-implicit dense mapping, and foundation model-enabled robotics. See you in Vienna!
